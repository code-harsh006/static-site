About Falkon Future X

Pioneering sustainable solutions for India's plastic waste crisis

Our Mission

We're committed to transforming India's plastic waste landscape through innovative recycling technologies, community education, and sustainable business models that create both environmental and economic value.

The Challenge

India generates over 3.5 million tons of plastic waste annually, with only 60% being collected and recycled. Our mission is to bridge this gap and create a circular economy for plastics.
